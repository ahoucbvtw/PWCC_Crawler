  • Sold Price filter is defaulted to $50 (can be moved to $0 if desired).
  • Sale Type filter is defaulted to auction sales only (can include fixed-price sales if desired).
  • Unpaid items are flagged and removed from the database since they donâ€™t represent a true sale on the marketplace.
  • Listings with the word 'reprint' in the title are automatically removed.
  • Images are only available for sales after early March 2018.
